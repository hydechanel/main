About

Harvey Jepson (Hyde Chanel) is an aspiring electronic dance music artist, his main focus is creating deep minimal tech house, along with spacey ambient soundscapes. Harvey is intuitively drawn to designing sound that engenders themes of vastness and deep consciousness, as well as music that evokes a sense of calming optimism that makes you dance, with hip-hop and jazz influences. Harvey has always been fascinated by art and technology, especially sound design and film scores from classic 2000s animation and retro video games. His main influences today are Mike Sharon & Robin Rafa, Aron Volta (Electronic Dance Music Producers), Hans Zimmer (Film Composer) and Brian Eno (Ambient Music Producer).
